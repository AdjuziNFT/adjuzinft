Ukrainaid

In february 2022, a military conflict started that is currently shaking the world. It is the conflict between Russia and Ukraine.
Several countries have sent military aid in the form of supplies and weapons to Ukraine, but not much have been written (in comparison) about the aid to normal citizens and refugees of Ukraine.

After spending countless hours everyday updating news sites to update myself on the conflict I thought about how I could help. My interest in crypto currencies and knowledge about web design made me set up this website and a crypto wallet. Donations to the wallet can be made with the currencies listed below and the donations will be used to help humanitarian organizations aid the people and refugees of Ukraine. The donations will not go to funding weapons and the like. There is an article on cryptonews.com that brings up several ways to donate if you want to support armed forces.
